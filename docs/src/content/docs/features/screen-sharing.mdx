Screen sharing is one of Hopp's core features, allowing you to share your screen
and allow for remote control of your computer by your teammates.

### How to share your screen

1. **Start a call or join a room**
2. **Click the screen share button** in the control panel
3. **Choose what to share** when a pop-up appears
4. Voila!
